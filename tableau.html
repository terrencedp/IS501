Comparing all 50 states in the United States, California has the highest profit of $76,381. On the other hand, Texas has the lowest profit among all the states in the United States with -$25,729. The map above shows the darker the color the more profit per state and the lighter the color it means that a state has less profit.


State with the Highest Profit

California is the top state for receiving most profit. The graph above shows the last two years of profit by month. There are up and down movement throughout the two years. We can still conclude that overall California is doing better from $2,891 in 2012 and finishing with $3,868 in 2014.
